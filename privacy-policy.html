Privacy Policy for Continuum - Daily Habits

Last updated: October 23, 2025

Introduction

Continuum - Daily Habits ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We are proud to say that Continuum - Daily Habits does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, we do not:

  • Collect any personal data or user information
  • Track your usage patterns or analytics
  • Store your habit data on external servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Require user accounts or registration

Local Data Storage

All your habit data, including:

  • Habit names and descriptions
  • Completion dates and streaks
  • Progress tracking information

is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

No Internet Connection Required

Continuum - Daily Habits is designed to work completely offline. The app does not require an internet connection to function, and we do not transmit any data over the internet.

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks that would collect your information.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your personal information through our app.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
