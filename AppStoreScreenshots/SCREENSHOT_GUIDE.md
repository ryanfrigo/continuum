## How to Capture Screenshots

### Using Simulator:
1. Run the app in Xcode with iPhone 16 Pro Max simulator
2. Navigate to each screen
3. Press `Cmd + S` in Simulator to save screenshot
4. Or use: `xcrun simctl io booted screenshot filename.png`

### Using Physical Device:
1. Press Side Button + Volume Up simultaneously
2. Screenshots save to Photos app

## Screenshot Tips for App Store

1. **Clean status bar**: Use Simulator for consistent time (9:41 AM is Apple's default)
2. **Full battery icon**: Simulator shows full battery by default
3. **No notifications**: Clear notification center
4. **Consistent data**: Use realistic-looking habit data with varied streaks
5. **Dark mode**: The app is dark-themed, ensure screenshots show this clearly

## Recommended Screenshot Order for App Store

### Description
Build lasting habits with Continuum, the beautifully designed habit tracker that guides you through the 66-day journey to habit formation.

**Key Features:**

• **66-Day Grid** - Visual progress tracking based on habit formation science
• **Health Score** - See your consistency percentage at a glance
• **Streak Tracking** - Watch your momentum build day by day
• **Milestone Celebrations** - Unlock achievements at 7, 21, 66, and 100 days
• **Clean Design** - Dark, minimal interface that stays out of your way
• **Quick Check-in** - Tap to mark habits complete in seconds

Research shows it takes an average of 66 days to form a lasting habit. Continuum keeps you focused on this journey with a simple, beautiful interface.

Start with suggested habits like Exercise, Read, Meditate, or create your own. Track your progress with the visual 66-day grid, and celebrate your milestones along the way.

No accounts required. No subscriptions. Just you and your habits.
